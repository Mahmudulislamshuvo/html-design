ABOUT ME

My Artistic Journey

For me, tattooing isn't just a job; it's a collaboration. I started drawing before I could walk, and that passion for art led me from canvas to skin. I've spent over a decade honing my craft, focusing on how to make ink flow with the body, how to create pieces that not only look good today but age gracefully for decades to come.

My studio is a private, appointment-only space designed for comfort and creativity. My goal is to provide a safe, professional, and inspiring environment where we can work together to create something truly permanent and personal.

YOUR SAFETY IS MY PRIORITY

A Clean & Safe Studio

There is no compromise when it comes to hygiene. I treat my studio with the same standards as a medical facility to ensure your complete safety and peace of mind.

  • 100% disposable, single-use needles and tubes.
  • Hospital-grade autoclave sterilization for all non-disposable equipment.
  • All surfaces disinfected with medical-grade virucide before and after each client.
  • Certified in Bloodborne Pathogens & Infection Control (OSHA Compliant).

Your commitment to aftercare is a commitment to the art itself.
