Featured Case Study

A deep dive into how I solved a complex problem for a recent client.

Fintech Startup

Redesigning an Investment App

The Challenge

The client's investment app was powerful but suffering from high user drop-off during onboarding. User feedback cited a "confusing" and "overwhelming" interface.

My Solution & Process

I initiated a 2-week design sprint, starting with user interviews to pinpoint exact friction points. I then simplified the user flow, designed a clean, component-based UI, and created an interactive prototype in Figma. We user-tested the prototype with 5 target users, iterated on feedback, and delivered a production-ready design system.

The Outcome

The new design led to a 40% increase in onboarding completion and a significant rise in positive App Store reviews.
